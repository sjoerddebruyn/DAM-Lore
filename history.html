Historical Entries

Benton Hall

Location: Oregon State University Campus

Date Established: 1889

Benton Hall is one of the oldest buildings on campus, representing the university's architectural history and long-standing traditions.

Gill Coliseum

Location: Oregon State University Campus

Date Established: 1949

Home to the OSU Beavers basketball team, Gill Coliseum stands as a testament to athletic excellence and school spirit.

Memorial Union

Location: Oregon State University Campus

Date Established: 1928

The Memorial Union is the heart of student activity and a vibrant space that brings the OSU community together.

Weatherford Hall

Location: Oregon State University Campus

Date Established: 1928

Weatherford Hall is a historic dormitory that has housed generations of OSU students and played a central role in campus life.

Reser Stadium

Location: Oregon State University Campus

Date Established: 1953

Reser Stadium hosts OSU football games and is a centerpiece of Beaver pride and athletic history.
